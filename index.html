Hospital CFOs today are navigating one of the most financially demanding periods in recent history. With health system operating margins hovering near 1–2% and non-labor expenses climbing above 5% year-over-year, every budget assumption, every forecast, every capital planning decision carries weight. StrataJazz® exists precisely to give healthcare finance leaders the data infrastructure to make those decisions with confidence — and I want to be the analyst who builds that infrastructure with them.

I am applying for the Analyst, Planning & Performance Management role at Strata Decision Technology. With an MS in Business Analytics from DePaul University (4.0 GPA), hands-on SAP ERP experience, a direct healthcare operations background, and a track record of translating complex data into financial decision tools, I bring the exact combination of finance domain depth, implementation rigor, and analytics capability this role demands.

At Morningstar, I analyzed and standardized 390,000+ investment portfolio records using SQL and Python, reduced downstream analysis time by 30%, and designed Power BI and Tableau dashboards that surfaced data-quality gaps used directly in governance discussions. The work required exactly what StrataJazz implementations demand: translating business requirements into validation logic, configuring reporting outputs, and communicating findings to stakeholders making high-stakes decisions.

I built Power BI dashboards via SAP ERP to track revenue and operational KPIs, and reengineered cash-flow forecasting models that improved accuracy by 12%. This is not adjacent experience to what Strata's clients need — it is the experience.

At Macleods Pharmaceuticals, I partnered with finance stakeholders to write BRDs and User Stories for reporting automation, applied SAP ERP analysis to reduce payment delays by 15%, and automated reconciliation workflows that shortened the month-end close by 1.5 days. I understand how enterprise finance systems behave in real organizations — and how to close the gap between what software can do and what clients actually need.

Strata's 2025 Snowflake partnership — building one of the largest comparable healthcare financial databases in the U.S. — signals a platform that is accelerating, not standing still. I am eager to grow alongside that acceleration, contributing to Capital Planning, Operational Budgeting, and Performance Management implementations as Strata extends its lead in healthcare enterprise performance management.

I am based in Chicago, deeply aligned with Strata's mission, and ready to deliver client value from day one. I would welcome the opportunity to speak with your team.
